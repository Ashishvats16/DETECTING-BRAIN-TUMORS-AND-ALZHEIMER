BRAIN TUMOR AND ALZHIEMER'S
DETECTION

Our website is dedicated to providing advanced diagnostic solutions for the early
detection of Alzheimer's disease and brain tumors. Our mission is to improve patient outcomes
by enabling timely interventions and personalized treatment plans.

Why early detection matters?

Alzheimer's disease and brain tumors are complex neurological conditions
that require prompt attention. Early detection plays a crucial role in improving
the quality of life for affected individuals. It allows for timely medical interventions,
access to appropriate treatments, and the opportunity to plan for the future.

The purpose of our website.

The purpose of our website is to raise awareness about brain tumors
and Alzheimer's disease while showcasing the potential of Convolutional
Neural Networks (CNN) and Python in early detection and improved
patient outcomes. We provide comprehensive information on these conditions,
explain CNN technology and its application in medical imaging analysis,
present our project methodology, and demonstrate the accuracy of our models.
Through collaboration and innovation, we strive to advance medical research
and make a positive impact on the lives of patients worldwide.
Join us on this journey of exploring cutting-edge solutions in healthcare.

Technologies we used.

WebD

WebD involves creating and maintaining websites and web apps. It combines HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for structure, style, and interactivity. Server-side scripting languages like PHP handle logic and database integration. Frameworks like React and Angular streamline development.

Machine Learning

Machine Learning is a subfield of artificial intelligence (AI) that focuses on developing algorithms and models that enable computers to learn and make predictions or decisions without being explicitly programmed. ML algorithms learn from data and improve their performance over time through experience.

Deep Learning

Deep Learning is a subset of Machine Learning that involves the use of artificial neural networks with multiple layers to learn and extract high-level representations from complex data. These neural networks, called deep neural networks, are designed to simulate the human brain's structure and function.

CNN

Convolutional Neural Networks are a type of deep neural network specifically designed for processing structured grid-like data, such as images or sequential data. CNNs are particularly effective in computer vision tasks, as they can automatically learn and extract hierarchical representations from images.
